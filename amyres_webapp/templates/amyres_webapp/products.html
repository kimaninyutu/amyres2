AGReFIND: Bridging the Gap Between Agricultural Research and Farmers.

AGReFIND is an AI-powered mobile app that connects stakeholders to localized agricultural research, ensuring access to actionable, up-to-date information, driving the adoption of effective agricultural innovations.

Target users: Smallholder farmers, Large scale farmers, researcers, NGOs, other agribusinesses.

Goal: Increase the adoption of agricultural research findings among smallholder farmers, commercial farmers, NGOs, and research institutions in Kenya.
